TV Activation Guide

  • Power on your Smart TV or streaming device.
  • Connect your device to a stable Wi-Fi network.
  • Go to the device “Settings” and select “Activation / Accounts”.
  • Sign in with your account or create a new one.
  • Enter the activation code displayed on your TV screen.
  • Complete the setup and start streaming.
